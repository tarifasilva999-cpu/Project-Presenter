# Project-Presenter
## Presenter 4.0 (BETA)

Presenter 4.0 is a lightweight, community-focused app designed to help small churches run worship lyrics, slides, and Bible verses smoothly. It’s currently in **BETA**, which means it’s still a work in progress—but it’s functional and ready for early testers to try out!

---

## Why Try Presenter 4.0?

- Designed specifically for **small church teams and volunteers**.
- Run lyrics, slides, and Bible verses all in one place.
- Clean, simple interface—no clutter, no nonsense.
- Open to feedback: your ideas can directly shape the final release.

---

## Early Access / Support

Presenter 4.0 is free to download and use. If you like what you see and want to **support development**, you can contribute voluntarily. Supporters help fund updates, improvements, and future features—but **everyone gets the same app**, so no worries about sharing copies.

**Ways to support:**

- Donate $5–$20 to help keep development moving.
- Join the community: provide feedback, suggest features, and vote on new ideas.
- Share your experience with your church team and friends!

> Your support isn’t buying software—it’s helping shape Presenter 4.0 into something amazing for everyone.

---

## Getting Started

1. Download the latest BETA release from [Releases](#).  
2. Open the app and follow the on-screen instructions.  
3. Start running lyrics and slides instantly—feedback welcome!
